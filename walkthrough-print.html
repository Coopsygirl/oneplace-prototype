One Place — Prototype Walkthrough

This PDF contains live-rendered pages from the local prototype. Each page is shown with a short caption describing what to demo.

Child — Today

What to show:
  • Now card with micro-tasks (checkbox)
  • Next transport card with ETA
  • Later list for scheduled activities
  • Help Me quick access
Tip: refresh to see parent-added activities.

Child — My Week

What to show:
  • Week overview with day blocks

Child — Help Me

What to show:
  • Quick feelings buttons (Everything feels too much, I'm angry, etc.)
  • Immediate calming suggestions
Selecting a feeling logs an occurrence the parent can view.

Child — Travel / My Bus

What to show:

Parent — Dashboard

What to show:
